Good to know

A few honest answers.

Can Meet My Menu confirm that a dish is safe for an allergy?

Meet My Menu brings possible allergen concerns to your attention. It highlights the ingredients and allergen information it finds so you know what to ask about and can confirm it with restaurant staff.

Where does the menu information come from?

You can scan a paper menu, search for a restaurant, or paste a menu link. Meet My Menu tells you when information may be incomplete or inferred.

Do I need to download anything?

No. Meet My Menu opens in your phone's web browser. Use the “Open the app” link whenever you want to start.
